# 🏥 MediPredict AI

AI-Powered Clinical Decision Support System for Diabetes & Symptom-Based Health Screening

---

## 📌 Overview

MediPredict AI is a modern healthcare web application developed using:

- Streamlit
- Machine Learning
- Gemini AI
- SQLite
- Plotly

The system assists doctors and healthcare staff in:

- Diabetes risk prediction
- Symptom-based AI health analysis
- Patient record management
- AI-powered medical guidance
- PDF clinical report generation
- Interactive healthcare visualization

---

# ✨ Features

## 🔐 Authentication System

- Login & Signup
- Role-Based Access Control
- Admin / Doctor / Receptionist Roles

---

## 🧠 AI Diabetes Prediction

Machine Learning model predicts diabetes risk using:

- Glucose
- Blood Pressure
- Skin Thickness
- Insulin
- BMI
- Diabetes Pedigree Function
- Age

---

## 📊 Explainable AI

Displays:
- Risk score
- Risk meter
- Risk factor graphs
- Health comparisons
- Patient-friendly explanations

---

## 🩺 AI Symptom-Based Health Assistant

Analyzes symptoms like:
- Chest pain
- Fatigue
- Frequent urination
- Headache
- Shortness of breath

Provides:
- Risk category
- Emergency detection
- AI explanation
- Next-step medical guidance

---

## 🤖 Gemini AI Chatbot

AI assistant can:
- Explain diabetes risk
- Suggest next steps
- Explain BMI & glucose
- Answer healthcare questions

---

## 📁 Patient Management

- Patient registration
- Patient records
- Search functionality
- Uploaded report storage

---

## 📄 PDF Clinical Reports

Automatically generates:
- Patient details
- Risk assessment
- Clinical recommendations
- AI screening result

---

## 📈 Doctor Dashboard

Interactive analytics:
- Risk distribution
- Patient trends
- Glucose vs Risk graph
- High-risk patient monitoring
